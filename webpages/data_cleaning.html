Data Cleaning

As a first step after collecting data, we dealt with cleaning it and focused on the following aspects:

  • Slang

Using an internet slang dictionary scraped from netlingo we translated internet slang into english words.


  • Misspellings

We then ran the tweets through a spell checker.


  • Removing hashtags & URLs

We extracted # and URLand added sentiment after loading the emoticon dataset from qdapDictionaries.
